Boiled Potatoes

Class boiled potatoes

Simple and warm boiled potatoes.

Simple to make.

Recipe Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Salt

Recipe Instructions

  1. Fill a pot with water.
  2. Prep your potatoes by washing and scrubbing them to get the dirt off.
  3. Salt your water. After salting, place your cleaned potatoes inside the pot and heat the pot.
  4. Bring the pot to a boil then reduce the heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender when piercing with a fork.
  5. Drain the water and plate your potatoes.
