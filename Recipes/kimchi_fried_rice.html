Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Spicy and savory fried rice made with kimchi as a main ingredient

Iconic Korean dish that can be made quick and easy.

Uses ingredients that can be easily obtained and found in the average person's fridge (maybe not the kimchi).

Recipe ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Kimchi
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Spam or any meat of your choice
  • Egg
  • Green onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sesame oil
