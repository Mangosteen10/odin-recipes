Kimchi Fried Rice Recipe

Spicy and savory fried rice made with kimchi as a main ingredient

Iconic Korean dish that can be made quick and easy.

Uses ingredients that can be easily obtained and found in the average person's fridge (maybe not the kimchi).

Recipe Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Kimchi
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Spam or any meat of your choice
  • Egg
  • Green onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sesame oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat up a pan and add the vegetable oil.
  2. Add the spam or meat of your choice and stir fry until cooked.
  3. Add the kimchi and stir-fry for around 1 minute.
  4. Add rice and gochujang. Stir-fry all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes.
  5. Cook the egg to desired liking.
  6. Plate the fried rice and egg. Add sesame oil on top and garnish with green onion.
